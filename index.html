About Me

I am a Professor at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data, Hefei University. I serve as a master's supervisor and have led multiple key research projects funded by the Anhui Provincial Natural Science Foundation and national programs. My research focuses on image segmentation, object detection and recognition, visual security and robustness, and multimodal image information processing.
